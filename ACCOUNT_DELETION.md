# Account deletion - Money Manager

> **Last updated:** 7 April 2026

This page explains how to ask us to **delete your login account and all app data** tied to it. For deleting only financial data while keeping your account, see [DATA_DELETION.md](DATA_DELETION.md). Our general privacy practices are in [PRIVACY_POLICY.md](PRIVACY_POLICY.md).

## How to request account deletion

1. Send an email to **joeboulos94@gmail.com** from the **same email address** you use to sign in to the app.
2. Use this subject line: **Account deletion request - Money Manager**
3. In the body, clearly state that you want your **account** and **all associated app data** deleted.

We use your sign-in email to verify the request. If you no longer have access to that inbox, say so in the email and be prepared to answer reasonable follow-up questions so we can confirm it is your account.

If you have an **active subscription**, cancel it in **Google Play** or the **Apple App Store**. We cannot close your store billing for you; deletion of app data does not always cancel store subscriptions.

## What we delete

After we process a verified request, we aim to remove:

- Your **user account** (authentication and profile tied to that login)
- **Financial and app data** stored for that user, such as:
  - Accounts and wallets
  - Transactions
  - Budgets
  - Savings goals and related activity
  - Bills and recurring entries
  - Loans and loan payments
  - Categories and similar configuration you created in the app
- Other records in our systems that exist **only** to support your use of the app for that account

The exact steps follow our backend configuration for a full account removal.

## What may still exist afterward

Some information may be **retained for a limited time** or by **third parties** under their own rules, for example:

- **Security, fraud prevention, and legal compliance** (short-lived server logs or backups until rotated)
- **App stores and payment providers** (purchase history, receipts) — see Google or Apple support if you need account-level changes there
- **Advertising or analytics SDKs** — only to the extent they already collected data under their policies

We do not promise that every third party will erase data on our timeline. We delete or anonymize what we control in active systems where the law allows.

## Timing and questions

We will respond and act within a **reasonable period** once we can verify your request (often on the order of days, depending on volume and complexity).
