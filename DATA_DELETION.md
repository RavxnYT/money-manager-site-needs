# Data deletion (keep your account) - Money Manager

> **Last updated:** 7 April 2026

This page is for when you want to **remove your financial and app data** from Money Manager but **keep your login** so you can continue using the app with a clean slate.

To remove your **entire account** (login and everything), use [ACCOUNT_DELETION.md](ACCOUNT_DELETION.md). General privacy information is in [PRIVACY_POLICY.md](PRIVACY_POLICY.md).

## Option A - In the app (preferred)

1. Open the app and go to **Settings**.
2. Find **Delete My Data** (you may need to confirm with your **password**).
3. Complete the steps shown on screen.

The confirmation dialog explains that this permanently removes items such as **accounts, transactions, savings, budgets, and categories** (and related records cleared by our servers for your user).

## Option B - Email request

If you cannot use the app:

1. Email **joeboulos94@gmail.com** from the **same address** you use to sign in.
2. Subject line: **Data deletion request - Money Manager**
3. In the body, ask us to **delete your app data but keep your login account**.

We will delete your financial and related app data from our active systems **without** removing your sign-in account, so you can sign in again.

## What we delete

Data deletion (in-app or by email) is intended to clear the same kind of information as a reset of your workspace: accounts, transactions, budgets, savings goals, bills, recurring items, loans, categories, and other records tied to your user that the service is built to remove for this action.

Exact scope is implemented in our backend and matches what the in-app **Delete My Data** action triggers.

## What may still exist

The same **limitations** as full account deletion can apply where third parties or short-lived logs are involved. See **“What may still exist afterward”** in [ACCOUNT_DELETION.md](ACCOUNT_DELETION.md).

**Subscriptions:** Deleting data does not cancel a **Play Store** or **App Store** subscription. Manage billing in your store account.
